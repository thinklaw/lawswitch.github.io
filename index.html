How does

this work?

1

Add the intake app into your website.

Getting started is easy, and you'll be up and running in minutes. Either start by linking straight to the brandable landing page that we provide for you, or simply add our embed code into your website and the app will work within your law firm's website.

2

Ask people to book an appointment,
or start their matter online.

Getting people to engage is best done by connecting the LawSwitch intake app to a call to action button in your webpages. Alternatively you can display the app as a badge that floats conveniently down the bottom of your webpages. You could even run a social media campaign and point it straight to your branded landing page!

3

The app handles the
intake process and booking.

The LawSwitch intake app is a chatbot "assistant" which provides a friendly and intuitive "humanistic" experience. The app can ask the right questions, make decisions, and book in appointments on your behalf - all automatically without you needing to do anything. It can be customised to match your law firm’s unique identity, language and area of law.

4

The app generates personalised
documents and sends emails.

Once the app has completed an intake process with someone it can automatically generate personalised documents such as file notes, letters and information packs, create meeting invites, and then send personalised emails with all the information to them and you.

5

You and your client are better
prepared for your meeting.

Fine tune the messaging, information and documentation to provide an engaging, instructional, and trustworthy online intake process. The LawSwitch app empowers your clients and your lawyers to be better organised and prepared for their meetings, and frees them up to provide greater value to your clients and your firm.
